The Rules

Rules strictly enforced :)

  • All beverages will be alcoholic. Shandy is prohibited.
  • All Applicants will move en mass at the instruction of the Adjudicator. There is a 5 minute window from the time the Adjudicator steps into the next Pub to allow for stragglers. Should Applicants not make it to the next Pub on the route in the 5 minute window they will incur a shot fine.
  • Spilling your drink or the drink of a fellow Applicant will incur a shot fine.
  • Slow drinking will not be tolerated, and will lead to a shot fine.
  • Drinking the drink of another Applicant will result in a shot fine.
  • Singing is punishable by way of a shot fine; this is at the Adjudicators discretion.
